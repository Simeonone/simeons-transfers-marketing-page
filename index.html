Operations

Eliminating the unnecessary so that the necessary may speak.

Sell any player in your team instantly to any available club at a fee

With the player initials, price and club initials, you can sell any player that is available in your club to any other club. Doing so will update your UI, and the buying club's UI, and you can sort your transactions accordingly

Buy the player of your dreams from any club at a price

With the player initials and price, you can buy any player that is available in other clubs into your club. Doing so will update your UI and the selling club's UI, and you can sort your transactions accordingly

See all the players you have bought and sold in an organized fashion

Based on your transaction history, you can see all the information on players you have previously bought or sold, in terms of player name, type of transfer (in or out), date of transfer, and the cost of each transfer. In addition, you can sort the transaction history according to transfer fee.
